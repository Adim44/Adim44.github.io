The Adim Ranch Leisure Park is an entertainment facility in Kiganjo, Nyeri County. Open daily including public holidays and only a two hour drive from Nairobi City Centre. The Leisure Park is a beautiful and intimate haven for outdoor lovers, set on an expansive space between the breath taking views of Mount Kenya and the Aberdare Mountain Range. Chaka Ranch also boasts unique outdoor entertainment facility comprising of the biggest and widest collection of quad bikes and buggies in Kenya. We offer a one-stop point for family fun, outdoor activity, food, drinks and mouth-watering barbecues. Welcome to Chaka Ranch for an unforgettable experience.
